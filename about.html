About ESPL

Our company ESPL commenced operations in July 2020 and is a Govt of India recognised Start-Up, based in Mumbai. We are a group of Technology and Behavioural Science enthusiasts – IIT, XLRI & TISS alumni, with over 100 years of collective professional experience, focussed on bringing the Internet of Behaviours technology and Behavioural Neuroscience together. We offer a modular, Behaviour-based platform to help our customers drive specific behaviour-based growth priorities.

Our product TRU360 is an outcome of more than 4 years of our research, active experimentation in live enterprise environments, professional experience and advice from top practitioners in the industry. Tru360 can operate stand-alone or as a behaviour based force multiplier bolted onto your existing digital tools

Tru 360

Leverage the power of IoB (Internet of Behaviours) technologies, most suited to operate Feedback Processes.

Maintain the 360 data for future applications.
